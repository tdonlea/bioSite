Who Is Terri Donlea?

Bio

Terri Donlea is an ambitious and down-to-earth Elementary P.E. teacher who doubles as an Instagram Influencer in her free-time outside of school. Growing up, she spent most of her early childhood in Minnesota, and then moved to California where she graduated high school. After graduation, she moved to Iowa to be closer to family; where she got her Bachelor’s degree in education at the University of Northern Iowa. Terri got married to the love of her life, Nick Donlea, in 1997. They have three beautiful children: one son named Cameron who is 32 and works as a CNC machinist in Iowa, a daughter named Haley who is 27 and is a front end manager at a home improvement store, and another daughter, Taylor who is 25 and works at Target while working towards her B.S. in software development. In Terri’s free time, she enjoys home innovation projects, working on her social media site, and spending time with her husband and two mini labradoodles, Wrigley and Ivy. She also enjoys traveling and discovering new restaurants in the area, as well as being active and being outdoors (as long as the Iowa weather cooperates that day). When asked if she has any regrets in her life, she quickly answered ‘absolutely zero’ because every single thing, good and bad, that has happened to her has gotten her exactly where she is today. When asked what her biggest accomplishments are, she responded with her children, her house, and either her renovated back patio or her downstairs living area renovation. Terri’s biggest motivators in life are her children, as well as showing the world what she (and her husband) are capable of in regards to being an interior/exterior designer.
